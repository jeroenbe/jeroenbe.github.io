Jeroen Berrevoets

I’m a 3rd year PhD student at the University of Cambridge where I am a member of the vanderschaar-lab. My research is focused on causality and machine learning, sometimes applied to medicine. I am supervised by prof. Mihaela van der Schaar. Previously I’ve spent time at VUB, KULeuven, and BMW Group. If you want to reach out, please send an email to jeroen [dot] berrevoets [at] maths.cam.ac.uk.
